Frequently Asked Questions

ROUVOO is an e-commerce platform designed to simplify your daily life by offering a wide range of services, including food delivery, gadgets, cleaning services, and bill payments, all in one seamless, user-friendly application.

We provide food delivery, gadget shopping, cleaning services, and bill payment options, ensuring convenience and efficiency for our users.

Download the ROUVOO app from your app store, create an account, and explore the range of services we offer.

ROUVOO is currently available in Akure and its environs with plans to expand to other regions in the near future.

We partner with trusted vendors and service providers, and all transactions are monitored to ensure high-quality and reliable service.

Yes, the ROUVOO app provides real-time tracking for your orders and service requests, keeping you updated every step of the way.

We accept multiple payment methods, including debit/credit cards, bank transfers, and in-app wallets.

Delivery fees may vary depending on your location and the service selected. The exact fee will be displayed during checkout.

Our customer support team is available to assist you. Simply contact us via the app or email, and we’ll resolve your issue promptly.

Yes, ROUVOO prioritises the security of your personal and payment information with advanced encryption and secure payment gateways.

Yes, we welcome partnerships with local businesses. Contact us to discuss how we can work together to provide exceptional services to our users.
